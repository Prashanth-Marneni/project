Artificial Intelligence(AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems .AI programming focuses on three cognitive skills: learning, reasoning and self-correction.Artificial Intelligence has brought a revolution in the world of technology.

The Art of developing websites for the Internet (World Wide Web) or Intranet (Private) is known as web development.It is basically divided into Front-end,Back-end and Full stack.A well-designed website is essential for reaching a larger audience and generating more leads or customers.

The Art of protecting the computer systems and websites against the unauthorised access and malicious attacks and ensuring the confidentiality, integrity is known as cybersecurity. Cyber Security aims to reduce the risk of cyber attacks and protect against the unauthorised exploitation of systems and networks.The field is becoming increasingly significant due to the increased reliance on computer systems and the Internet.
